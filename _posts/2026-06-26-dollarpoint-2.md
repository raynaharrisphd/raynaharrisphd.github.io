## Theme: Sensory Systems

Five Traditional Senses
- sight (vision)
- hearing (audition)
- smell (olfaction)
- taste (gustation)
- touch (somatosensation)

Five Additional senses
- body awareness (proprioception)
- balance (equilibrioception)
- temperature (thermoception)
- pain (nociception)
- internal state (interception)
- time (chronoception)
