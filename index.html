Hi! I'm Rayna. I was born in Texas, but now I live near Lake Tahoe in California. I have a Ph.D. in Molecular and Cellular Biology and a B.S in Biochemistry from The University of Texas at Austin. I am an Adjunct Faculty Member in Biology and Chemistry at Lake Tahoe Community College. I am a certified yoga instructor and teach outdoor yoga with The Courage Project and Tahoe Flow Arts & Fitness. I also work as a Mountain Host at Palisades Tahoe Alpine, a barista at The Crest Cafe, and a Host at The River Ranch.
