Yoga with Rayna

Rayna Harris, PhD, is an enthusiastic student and teacher of Hatha, Restorative, and Vinyasa, yoga, as well as a professor of biology and chemistry. She began practicing yoga regularly during graduate school as a tool for stress relief and overall well-being. Rayna deepened her knowledge with a 200-hour Hatha Yoga Teacher Training with the Yoga Alliance in 2023 at Palisades Yoga Studio in Olympic Valley. She teaches yoga with The Courage Project, Tahoe Flow Arts & Fitness, and the Sporthaus at Sugar Bowl. Rayna’s fun and welcoming classes guide students through sequences that synchronize the mind, body, and spirit. She especially loves teaching yoga on the beach or on stand-up paddleboards, where you can feel the warmth of the sun and hear the soothing sounds of the lake while you practice.
