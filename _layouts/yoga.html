Yoga

After daydreaming about teaching yoga for years, I finally pursued and received my 200-Hour Hatha Teacher Yoga certification in January 2024 at Palisades Yoga in North Lake Tahoe. My yoga classes flow through poses and synchronize the breath with movement and music in a playful way that builds physical strength and encourages mindfulness. I teach Beach Yoga, Standup Paddle Yoga, and Yoga at the Ritz Carlton with Tahoe Flow Arts & Fitness and Mindfull Yoga for children with The Courage Project. I write a blog post for every class that includes an outline and/or image of the sequence I planned to teach, a link to the Spotify playlist, and feedback from students. Scroll down to read more.
