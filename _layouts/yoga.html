Yoga with Rayna

I teach mindful yoga outside (at the beach and on standup paddle boards) and indoors with Tahoe Flow Arts & Fitness and The Courage Project. My yoga classes flow through poses and synchronize the breath with movement and music in a playful way that builds physical strength and encourages mindfulness. I write a blog posts for many of the yoga classes I teach. I include outline of the sequence, a link to relevant Spotify playlists and YouTube vidos, and general notes or feedback from students.
